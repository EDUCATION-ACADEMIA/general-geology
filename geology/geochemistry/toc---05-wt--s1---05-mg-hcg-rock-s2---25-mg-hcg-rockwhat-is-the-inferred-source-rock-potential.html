Concept note-1: -Based on the established source rocks quality values (Table 3), it can be noticed that if the TOC of source rock is greater than 1 percent, the rock can be a good source rock. If the rock contains greater than 4 percent Total Organic Carbon, then it has an excellent petroleum potential.

Concept note-2: -Total organic carbon (TOC) is the main measurement used for estimating the quality and productivity of a source rock. The source rock-usually an organic rich shale-contains various carbon compounds such as kerogen, bitumen, oil and gas.

Concept note-3: -The source rocks satisfy three geochemical requirements such as quantity (amount of organic matter), quality (type of organic matter or kerogen type) and thermal maturity (extent of burial heating) (Peters and Cassa, 1994) .
