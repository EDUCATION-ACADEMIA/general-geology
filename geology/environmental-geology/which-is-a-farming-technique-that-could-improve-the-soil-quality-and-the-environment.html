Which is a farming technique that could improve the soil quality and the environment?

(A) using fueled machines that will turn the soil continuously

(B) irrigate the crops using well water

(C) ** rotate the types of crops grown

(D) plant only genetically modified crops

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Crop rotation is the agronomic practice of growing crops on the same paddock in sequence. It has several benefits for soil and crop systems. Beneficial effects include lower incidence of weeds, insects, and plant diseases, as well as improvements of soil physical, chemical, and biological properties.

Concept note-2: -The process of loosening and turning of soil is called Tilling or ploughing. It is done by using a plough. The practice of cultivating plants and livestock along with crop production is called agriculture. The annual practice of growing crops and harvesting it is called agricultural practice.

Concept note-3: -In intensive type of farming crops are rotated.

Concept note-4: -Crop Rotation Question 5 Detailed Solution. Green manures are the crops that are grown primarily for building and maintaining soil fertility and structure. Green manure crops should be such that they can be grown quickly.
