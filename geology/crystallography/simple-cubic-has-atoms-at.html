simple cubic has atoms at

(A) ** corners

(B) centre of body

(C) centre of surface

(D) nowhere

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In a simple cubic unit cell since each cubic unit cell has 8 atoms on its corners.

Concept note-2: -Assertion (A): The total number of atoms present in a simple cubic unit cell is one. Reason (R): Simple cubic unit cell has atoms at its corners, each of which is shared between eight adjacent unit cells.

Concept note-3: -A cubic unit cell has one atom on each corner and one atom on each body diagonal.

Concept note-4: -There are only 8 corners so, no. of atoms per unit cell = 8×18=1. Step by step solution by experts to help you in doubt clearance & scoring excellent marks in exams.

Concept note-5: -The corners contribute only one net atom and the center atom contributes another for a total of two net atoms. Please, notice that the corner atoms are not touching one another. Instead, the atoms touch through the center atom along the diagonal of the cube.
