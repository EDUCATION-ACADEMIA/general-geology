select incorrect statement for crystallography

(A) Crystallography is the experimental science of the arrangement of atoms in solids.

(B) It is the branch of science concerned with the structure and properties of crystals

(C) Something is crystalline if the atoms or ions that compose it are arranged in a regular way

(D) ** all are incorrect

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Statement 2 : In NaHCO3 and HCO-3 ions are linked together through intermolecular hydrogen bonds into an infinite chain, while in KHCO3, HCO−3 ions form dimeric anions through intermolecular hydrogen bonds. Both Statement 1 and Statement 2 are true.

Concept note-2: -We should know that in zinc blende structure (ZnS), sulphide ions are face-centred and zinc is present in alternate tetrahedral voids. Formula is ZnS. Coordination number of Zn is 4 and that of sulphide is 4. So, from this we can say that this statement is incorrect.

Concept note-3: -Solution : Each Cs ion is surrounded by 8 ‘Cl^(-)‘ ions and each ‘Cl^(-)‘ ion is surrounded by 8 ‘Cs^(+)‘ ions. CsCl has a bcc type structure. Thus, the structure has 8:8 coordination number.

Concept note-4: -Cation, Cs+ is at the body centre of the cube and all anions are at the corner of the cube.
