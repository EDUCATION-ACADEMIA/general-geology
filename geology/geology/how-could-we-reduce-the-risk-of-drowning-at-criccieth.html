How could we reduce the risk of drowning at Criccieth?

(A) ** Keep at least 5 meters from the sea.

(B) ** Work in groups.

(C) Do not go on the beach.

(D) Wear suitable footwear.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Not wearing life jackets. Life jackets can prevent drowning during water activities, especially boating and swimming. The U.S. Coast Guard reported 658 boating-related deaths in 2021-81% died by drowning, and 83% of these people were not wearing life jackets.

Concept note-2: -Learn basic swimming and water safety skills. Formal swimming lessons can reduce the risk of drowning. Build fences that fully enclose pools. Supervise closely. Wear a life jacket. Learn CPR. Know the risks of natural waters. Avoid alcohol. Use the buddy system. More items

Concept note-3: -Lack of swimming ability. The most common cause of drowning is not knowing how to swim. No barriers around the pool. Lack of supervision. Failure to wear life jackets. Alcohol use. 19-Jun-2013
