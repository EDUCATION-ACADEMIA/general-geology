A ____ is a set of interacting parts forming a complex whole.

(A) geologic process

(B) landform

(C) channel

(D) ** system

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A system is a group of interrelated interacting, or interdependent parts that for a complex whole. Most of us hear and use the term frequently.

Concept note-2: -Systems A system is an organized group of interacting parts. A cell is a system of chemicals and processes. A body system includes organs that interact. An ecosystem includes living and nonliving things that interact.

Concept note-3: -A system is a group of interacting, interrelated and interdependent components that form a complex and unified whole. These independent and possibly distributed systems pool their resources together, creating a new and more complex system.

Concept note-4: -Answer and Explanation: A system is an organized group of related parts that interact to form a whole. The complex network which connects several biological relevant entities is called a system. A system pertains to interdependent or interrelated objects comprising and functioning as a whole.
