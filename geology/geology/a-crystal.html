A crystal____

(A) Is shiny(lustrous)

(B) has an orderly pattern of atoms, which gives it a patterned look

(C) can be valuable

(D) ** is all of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -crystal, any solid material in which the component atoms are arranged in a definite pattern and whose surface regularity reflects its internal symmetry.

Concept note-2: -Crystal structure: It is an ordered arrangement of atoms, ions, or molecules in a crystalline material. Common crystal structures are. (1) Simple cubic structure: In a simple cubic structure ‘8’ atoms are arranged in 8 corners of the unit cell and they will be shared by 3 nearest corner atoms within the unit cell.

Concept note-3: -The scientific definition of a “crystal” is based on the microscopic arrangement of atoms inside it, called the crystal structure. A crystal is a solid where the atoms form a periodic arrangement.

Concept note-4: -A crystal is a homogeneous solid in which particles (atoms, molecules or ions) are arranged in a definite pattern due to which they have a definite geometrical shape with a plane surface. For example: Sodium chloride and sugar both are crystalline with a definite cubical shape for their crystals.
