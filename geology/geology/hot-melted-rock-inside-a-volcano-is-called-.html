Hot melted rock inside a volcano is called ____

(A) dragon fire

(B) ** magma

(C) lava

(D) cement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Scientists use the term magma for molten rock that is underground and lava for molten rock that breaks through the Earth’s surface.

Concept note-2: -Igneous rock (derived from the Latin word ignis meaning fire), or magmatic rock, is one of the three main rock types, the others being sedimentary and metamorphic. Igneous rocks are formed through the cooling and solidification of magma or lava.

Concept note-3: -Igneous rocks are called ‘’fire rocks” because they are formed from molten rock that is beneath the surface of Earth. The molten rock within the surface is known as magma.

Concept note-4: -The hottest lava around today is deemed “mafic, ” a type that contains mineral compositions that ensure it melts at the highest temperatures.
