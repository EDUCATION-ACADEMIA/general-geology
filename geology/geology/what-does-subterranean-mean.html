What does subterranean mean?

(A) ** under the earth

(B) in the earth

(C) over the earth

(D) on the earth

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -: being, lying, or operating under the surface of the earth. : existing or working in secret : hidden.

Concept note-2: -adjective Also sub·ter·ra·ne·ous. existing, situated, or operating below the surface of the earth; underground.

Concept note-3: -A subterranean river or tunnel is under the ground. The city has many subterranean passages.

Concept note-4: -Subterranea are underground structures, both natural (such as caves) and human-made (such as mines).

Concept note-5: -under the ground: subterranean passages. a subterranean river. SMART Vocabulary: related words and phrases.
