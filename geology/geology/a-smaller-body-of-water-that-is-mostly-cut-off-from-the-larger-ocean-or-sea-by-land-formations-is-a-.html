A smaller body of water that is mostly cut off from the larger ocean or sea by land formations is a ____

(A) a sea

(B) ** a gulf

(C) a bay

(D) a lake

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bay is generally smaller than a gulf. A Lagoon is a part of a shallow body of water which is seperated naturally by a large body of water.

Concept note-2: -Pool – various small bodies of water such as a swimming pool, reflecting pool, pond, or puddle. Pond – a body of water smaller than a lake, especially those of artificial origin.

Concept note-3: -Seas are smaller than oceans and are usually located where the land and ocean meet. Typically, seas are partially enclosed by land.

Concept note-4: -The coast, also known as the coastline or seashore, is defined as the area where land meets the ocean, or as a line that forms the boundary between the land and the coastline. The Earth has around 620, 000 kilometres (390, 000 mi) of coastline.

Concept note-5: -The curved identations of the sea into the land with a wide opening is called a Bay.
