Which segment of time is the smallest?

(A) Eon

(B) Era

(C) Period

(D) ** Epoch

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Epoch: This is the smallest unit of geologic time. An Epoch lasts several million years.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The shortest geologic time frame is B. epochs. Geological time frames from longest to shortest include eons, eras, periods, epochs and ages.

Concept note-3: -era = A unit of time shorter than an eon but longer than a period. period = A unit of time shorter than an era but longer than epoch. epoch = A unit of time shorter than a period but longer than an age.

Concept note-4: -Some of the more famous epochs include the: Quaternary (current epoch), Cretaceous, Jurassic, Triassic, and Devonian. Epochs, also referred to as â€œseries", are the subdivisions of periods. Epochs tend to be a several million years in length. The current epoch is the Holocene.
