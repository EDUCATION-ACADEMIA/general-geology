Which of the following is NOT a landform?

(A) mountain

(B) channel

(C) sand dune

(D) ** lightning

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The three main kinds of landforms are mountains, plains and plateaus. Tides are a kind of ocean movement.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Eskers.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Cirque. The landform as a result of erosional and depositional action of running water are known as fluvial landforms. George, Braids, Canyon, Alluvial fans and Cones, Natural Levee, Waterfalls, potholes meanders, Oxbow lakes, and deltas are examples of fluvial landforms.

Concept note-4: -Mountains, hills, plateaus, and plains are the four major types of landforms. Minor landforms include buttes, canyons, valleys, and basins.
