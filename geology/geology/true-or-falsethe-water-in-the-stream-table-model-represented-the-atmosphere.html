True or False:The water in the Stream Table Model represented the atmosphere.

(A) True

(B) ** False

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This inturn will lead to the end of the life because world without plants means no food, no oxygen, not enough rain and other innumerable problems. Therefore, it is true that water is a precious resource.

Concept note-2: -Therefore, rain is not a source of water, it’s one of the stages of the life cycle of water. Hence this statement is false.

Concept note-3: -The atmosphere is the superhighway in the sky that moves water everywhere over the Earth. Water at the Earth’s surface evaporates into water vapor, then rises up into the sky to become part of a cloud which will float off with the winds, eventually releasing water back to Earth as precipitation.

Concept note-4: -Expert-verified answer The water table is not the same anywhere on the planet. The water table drops during the summer months, owing to plants sucking up water from the soil surface until it reaches the water table.
