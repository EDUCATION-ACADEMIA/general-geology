Which type of heat transfer is this an example of:tanning next to the ocean

(A) ** radiation

(B) conduction

(C) convection

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This is an example of radiation heat transfer.

Concept note-2: -As we know, that sun is present in space and there is no atmosphere i.e. no medium over there and the only mode of transfer of heat which do not require a medium is radiation. And by radiation only heat from sun is reached to Earth.

Concept note-3: -How Tanning Happens. The sunâ€™s rays contain two types of ultraviolet radiation that reach your skin: UVA and UVB. UVB radiation burns the upper layers of skin (the epidermis), causing sunburns. UVA radiation is what makes people tan.

Concept note-4: -Heat transfer by radiation occurs when microwaves, infrared radiation, visible light, or another form of electromagnetic radiation is emitted or absorbed. An obvious example is the warming of the Earth by the Sun. A less obvious example is thermal radiation from the human body.

Concept note-5: -Radiation is the transfer of heat energy through space by electromagnetic radiation. Most of the electromagnetic radiation that comes to the earth from the sun is invisible.
