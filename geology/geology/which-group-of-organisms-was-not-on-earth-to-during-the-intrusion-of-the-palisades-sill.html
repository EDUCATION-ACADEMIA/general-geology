Which group of organisms was not on earth to during the intrusion of the Palisades Sill?

(A) ** Birds

(B) Dinosaurs

(C) Mammals

(D) Corals

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2) Which fossil organisms could possibly be used as index fossils? The graptolite, placoderm, ammonite, ichthyosaur, and shark’s tooth could possibly be used as index fossils since they are found in only one layer.

Concept note-2: -The Ordovician and Cambrian Periods are referred to as the “age of invertebrates", with trilobites abundant. In this period, brachiopods became more abundant that the trilobites, but all but one species of them are extinct today.

Concept note-3: -Organisms that are least likely to be fossilized are ones that are made up of soft tissue (soft tissue does not mineralize) and that upon death are likely to decay or be eaten. They may also live in an environment that would make rapid sediment cover unlikely.

Concept note-4: -index fossil, any animal or plant preserved in the rock record of the Earth that is characteristic of a particular span of geologic time or environment. A useful index fossil must be distinctive or easily recognizable, abundant, and have a wide geographic distribution and a short range through time.
