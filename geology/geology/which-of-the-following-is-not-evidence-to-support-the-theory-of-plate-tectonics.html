Which of the following is NOT evidence to support the theory of plate tectonics?

(A) Fossils

(B) Mountains

(C) Glaciers

(D) ** Sand Erosion

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Which of the following statements is NOT supported by the plate tectonic theory? Continents move through the seafloor crust.

Concept note-2: -Answer and Explanation: The theory of plate tectonics has several supporting pieces of evidence from fossils, rock layers, and complementary coastlines. Fossilized organisms can be found on separate continents.

Concept note-3: -Evidence from fossils, glaciers, and complementary coastlines helps reveal how the plates once fit together.

Concept note-4: -They based their idea of continental drift on several lines of evidence: fit of the continents, paleoclimate indicators, truncated geologic features, and fossils.
