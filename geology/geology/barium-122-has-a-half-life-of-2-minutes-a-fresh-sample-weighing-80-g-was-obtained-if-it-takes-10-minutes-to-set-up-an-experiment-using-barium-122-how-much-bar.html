Barium-122 has a half-life of 2 minutes. A fresh sample weighing 80 g was obtained. If it takes 10 minutes to set up an experiment using barium-122, how much barium-122 will be left when the experiment begins?

(A) 0.25g

(B) ** 2.5g

(C) 25g

(D) 80g

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Barium-122 has a half-life of 2 minutes.

Concept note-2: -The half-life of iodine-131 is 8 days. Since the half-life is 8 days, 24 days corresponds to 3 half-lives.

Concept note-3: -As an example, iodine-131 is a radioisotope with a half-life of 8 days. It decays by beta particle emission into xenon-131. After eight days have passed, half of the atoms of any sample of iodine-131 will have decayed, and the sample will now be 50% iodine-131 and 50% xenon-131.

Concept note-4: -Iodine 13153I is used in diagnostic and therapeutic techniques in the treatment of thyroid disorders. This isotope has a half-life of 8.04 days.
