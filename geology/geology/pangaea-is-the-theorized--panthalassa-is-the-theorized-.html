Pangaea is the theorized ____ Panthalassa is the theorized ____

(A) original northern continent, original southern continent

(B) original southern continent, original northern Continent

(C) single continent hundreds of millions of years ago, single sea that surrounded it

(D) ** single sea hundreds of millions of years ago, single continent that it surrounded

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pangea, also spelled Pangaea, in early geologic time, a supercontinent that incorporated almost all the landmasses on Earth. Pangea was surrounded by a global ocean called Panthalassa, and it was fully assembled by the Early Permian Epoch (some 299 million to about 273 million years ago).

Concept note-2: -In 1912, German scientist Alfred Wegener proposed that Earth’s continents once formed a single, giant landmass, called Pangaea. Over millions of years, Pangaea slowly broke apart, eventually forming the continents as they are today.

Concept note-3: -Continental Drift Theory by Alfred Wegener According to Wegener’s hypothesis of continental drift, Pangaea, a Mega Ocean surrounding a supercontinent that consisted of all the continents as a single continental mass known as a Super Continent. Panthalassa is another name for the Mega Ocean.

Concept note-4: -About 200 million years ago, all the continents on Earth were actually one huge “supercontinent” surrounded by one enormous ocean. This gigantic continent, called Pangaea, slowly broke apart and spread out to form the continents we know today. All Earth’s continents were once combined in one supercontinent, Pangaea.
