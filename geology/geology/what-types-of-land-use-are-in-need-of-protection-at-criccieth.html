What types of land use are in need of protection at Criccieth?

(A) ** Hotels and BNBs.

(B) ** Restaurants and takeaways.

(C) ** Residential housing.

(D) Farms and grassland.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Residential land is used to house individuals in various groups. There are many different types of residential land, including low-density housing, medium-density housing, high-density housing, and mixed-use development. Each of these types is categorized by how many individuals can be living on the land.

Concept note-2: -Residential land use: this is housing where people live and takes up the most space in a city. Housing density is high close to the city centre, with a high proportion of flats and apartments. High-density, back-to-back terraced housing is typically found in the inner city, where homes for factory workers were built.

Concept note-3: -There are five main different types of land use: residential, agricultural, recreation, transportation, and commercial.

Concept note-4: -It represents the economic and cultural activities (e.g., agricultural, residential, industrial, mining, and recreational uses) that are practiced at a given place.
