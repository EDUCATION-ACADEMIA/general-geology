What is an individual plant or animal?

(A) ** organism

(B) mammal

(C) ancestors

(D) cave man

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -An organism generally refers to a single or individual living species, which exhibits all the properties of life. These can be a plant, animal, bird, insect and even a microbe.

Concept note-2: -Most biologists implicitly define an individual organism as “one genome in one body.” This definition is based on physiological and genetic criteria, but it is problematic for colonial organisms.

Concept note-3: -An individual is one organism and is also one type of organism (e.g., human, cat, moose, palm tree, gray whale, tapeworm, or cow in our example).

Concept note-4: -Answer:Organism an individual animal, plant, or single-celled life form.
