Which of these is an example of erosion

(A) Light Shinning onto a mountain

(B) Rain breaking down rock

(C) ** Wind blowing away sediment

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Since erosion can occur as a result of action of water or wind on the surface of the Earth, “wind blowing away sediment” is an example of erosion among the other options listed in the question.

Concept note-2: -Erosion by Wind Wind is a powerful agent of erosion. Aeolian (wind-driven) processes constantly transport dust, sand, and ash from one place to another. Wind can sometimes blow sand into towering dunes.

Concept note-3: -Example of erosion: Wind carries small pieces of rock away from the side of a mountain. Chemical Weathering: – Decomposition of rock and soil due to chemical reactions.

Concept note-4: -Erosion is the wearing away and removal of rock or sediment. Erosion occurs because gravity, ice, wind, and water sculpt Earth’s surface.
