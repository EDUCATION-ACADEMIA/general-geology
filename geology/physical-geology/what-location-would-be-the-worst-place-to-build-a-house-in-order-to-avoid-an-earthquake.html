(B) near the coast

(C) in the mountains

(D) in the Piedmont

Concept note-1: -Soft-story Buildings: Extremely popular as a means of conserving space, these structures with open ground floors used for parking have been proven to be vulnerable because they lack the support to withstand lateral forces that push the building from side to side.
