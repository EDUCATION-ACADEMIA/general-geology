INDIAN GEOLOGY

[SOURCES]
India is the worlds fourth strongest army

(A) ** true

(B) false

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the recently released Global Firepower (GFP) annual defence review of 140 countries, India’s military is the 4th most powerful.

Concept note-2: -The Global Firepower ranking is based on each nation’s potential defence capability across land, sea and air.

Concept note-3: -The Largest Armies in the World Include China>India> The United States>North Korea>Russia In Decreasing Order.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Army is seemingly the foundation of the Indian military. The troopers who watch India’s lines and tackle the security dangers of the country in the most testing circumstances have made the Indian Army one of the most toughest forces worldwide and fourth strongest in the world after US, Russia and China.

Concept note-5: -India’s Military Strength Ranking in the world is fourth. For 2022, India is ranked 4 out of the 142 countries that are considered for the Global GPF review. India holds a PwrIndx score of 0.0979.
