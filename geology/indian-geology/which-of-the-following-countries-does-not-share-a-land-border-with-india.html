Which of the following countries does not share a land border with India?

(A) China

(B) Myanmar

(C) ** Afganistan

(D) Bhutan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sri Lanka. India shares land borders with China, Bhutan, Nepal, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Afghanistan, and Myanmar.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. Pakistan, Iran, Turkmenistan, Tajikistan share border with Afghanistan. Russia is not adjacent to Afghanistan.

Concept note-3: -India shares land border of 106 km with Afghanistan. This is the shortest in length amongst all other neighbouring countries. Q. With which country does India share the shortest border?

Concept note-4: -The country which does not share a land boundary with India is ‘Sri Lanka’.

Concept note-5: -The country which does not share the boundary with India is Sri Lanka. The island country Sri Lanka lies in the Indian Ocean. The Palk Strait separates India and Sri Lanka. India and Sri Lanka have diplomatic relations between them.
