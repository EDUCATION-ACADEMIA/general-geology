Which one is the largest district by area wise in Kerala

(A) Kollam

(B) Ernakulam

(C) Thrissur

(D) ** Idukki

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Palakkad district is the largest district by area and Alappuzha District is the smallest district in Kerala.

Concept note-2: -Palakkad is the largest district of kerala covering area of 4482 km² while Alappuzha is the smallest district of kerala covering area of 1415 km². Malappuram is the most populous whereas Wayanad is the least populous district in kerala state.

Concept note-3: -Idukki which lies in the Western Ghats of Kerala is the second largest district in area but has the lowest population density. Idukki has a vast forest reserve area; more than a half of the district is covered by forests.

Concept note-4: -The largest and smallest district by area in the state are Palakkad and Alappuzha respectively whereas Malappuram and Wayanad are the most and least populous districts of the state respectively.
