In general, the relief of the earth’s surface is grouped into 3, namely ____

(A) ** Relief Order I, Relief Order II, Relief Order III

(B) Land Relief, Ocean Relief, Atmospheric Relief

(C) Top Surface Relief, Underwater Relief, Land Relief

(D) Old Relief, Adult Relief, Young Relief

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The third order of relief includes individual peaks, cliffs, valleys, hills, spurs, gorges, sand dunes, caves, moraines, cirques, ripples, beaches, etc.

Concept note-2: -First order relief features are tectonic plates and are the largest in special extent. There are two types of plates; continental plates and Oceanic plates. These are differentiated by their rock and mineral composition.

Concept note-3: -First order relief – refers to the coarsest level of landforms, including continental platforms and ocean basins. 2. 3. Third order relief – most detailed order of relief includes things like mountains, cliffs, valleys, hills, and other small scale landforms.

Concept note-4: -First order relief-The oceanic plates. Second order relief-Himalaya Mountains. Third Order Relief-Pawnee cirque.
