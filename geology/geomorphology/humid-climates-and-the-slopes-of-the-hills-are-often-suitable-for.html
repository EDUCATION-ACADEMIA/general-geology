Humid climates and the slopes of the hills are often suitable for____

(A) Nothing

(B) Only settlement

(C) Only farming

(D) ** Farming and human settlement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -terrace cultivation, method of growing crops on sides of hills or mountains by planting on graduated terraces built into the slope. Though labour-intensive, the method has been employed effectively to maximize arable land area in variable terrains and to reduce soil erosion and water loss.

Concept note-2: -Solution: Rubber requires a moist and humid climate with more than 200 cm rainfall and a temperature above 25°C. Rubber is an equatorial crop that can be additionally cultivated in tropical and subtropical areas.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Peaty soil. Peaty soil is found in areas of heavy rainfall and high humidity.

Concept note-4: -The plains are the most productive farming areas.
