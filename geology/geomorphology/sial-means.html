Sial means

(A) silicon and water

(B) ** silicon and aluminium

(C) silicon and nickel

(D) silicon and magnesium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -sial. / (ˈsaɪəl) / noun. the silicon-rich and aluminium-rich rocks of the earth’s continental upper crust, the most abundant individual rock being granite.

Concept note-2: -The uppermost layer of the crust is called the sial, consisting of silicate and aluminium (Si = silicate, Al = aluminium).

Concept note-3: -Expert-Verified Answer Aluminum Silicate is made of silicon and alumina.

Concept note-4: -The Continental Crust is also called the SiAl layer.

Concept note-5: -The name ‘sima’ was taken from the first two letters of silica and of magnesia. Comparable is the name ‘sial’, which is the name for the upper layer of Earth’s crust (continental crust).
