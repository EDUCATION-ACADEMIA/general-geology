The area of land drained by a river system including the ground water is known as a ____

(A) Watershed

(B) ** Catchment area

(C) Drainage basin

(D) Escarpment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A watershed is an entire river system-an area drained by a river and its tributaries. It is sometimes called a drainage basin.

Concept note-2: -A watershed, also called a drainage basin or catchment, is an area drained by a river and its tributaries.

Concept note-3: -1) An area from which surface runoff is carried away by a single drainage system. 2) The area of land bounded by watersheds draining into a river, basin or reservoir.

Concept note-4: -The area drained by a river along with all its tributaries is called a catchment area or river basin.

Concept note-5: -Catchment area: A river drains the water collected from a specific area, which is called its catchment area. River Basin: An area drained by a river and its tributaries is called a drainage basin. A river basin is made up of many different watersheds.
